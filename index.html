About the Project

This project focuses on the development of advanced techniques for geological data extraction in Sri Lanka using remote sensing. The goal is to implement data-driven algorithms to analyze openly accessible satellite datasets for the efficient and accurate identification of geological features.

By leveraging cutting-edge technology, spectral analysis methods, and openly available satellite data, the project aims to contribute to sustainable resource management and environmental monitoring, supporting geological mapping and research initiatives in Sri Lanka.
